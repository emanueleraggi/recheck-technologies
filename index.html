Our Solution

BOC is a small, portable and in-house testing kit for detecting ovarian cancer at the initial stage through a regular urine tester. BOC has the unique advantage of integrating bio-sensors into the tester lid. After turning the tester upside down, specific liquid and vapor sensors will analyze the bio content looking for specific traces related to ovarian cancer. After the analysis is finished, the smart lid connects to a smartphone that shows the results. The results can be sent both to a provider and to a physician for real-time assessment. BOC makes use of touchless technology. In fact it is enough to approach the smartphone to the tester lid to obtain a secure connection and see the results. BOC respects the anonymity of people at risk such as undocumented and low income who cannot afford to have a regular doctor. In this way the results of the test do not need to be sent and consulted officially. BOC is made by simple consumable electronics and it is environmentally friendly as it can be disposed of at the nearest pharmacy.
